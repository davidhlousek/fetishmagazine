“Who the F*@k are the Gs?

The Brno Gs stand as a local project that conduct podcasts (Via interviews) with local Gs (Including businesses, inspiring individuals from all areas of life). A “G” also known as a “Gangster” in modern urban terminology, might come off as a strong term for most of us. However, in our reference to a “G”, we mean a multitude of things that include all genders( Also Binary and non-binary), races and any individual from whatever walk of life. (mostly we are the Goofsters, as Vinny says) So really, anyone who’s capable, stands for what they believe in, and is trying to accomplish incredible things for the greater good, is by our definition a “G”. We operate as a quartet of misfits (Vincent, Lahiru, David and Rafael) that originally gave birth to Lotus AKA LOT(Of)US as a community based initiative to host events and festivals bringing together various groups of people from every corner of the creative communities in Brno. Some of our earlier work include events at Zahrádka Na Střeláku, SPOT Brno, Káznice Brno (Historical penitentiary of Brno).
