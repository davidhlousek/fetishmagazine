The magazine "Fetish" represents an effort to delve deeper into various aspects of fetish and fetishism through systematic research and discussions on the definition of fetishes. My approach focuses on individual perspectives, where each subject approaches the topic from their own experiences. Some view fetishism as merely a sexual preference, while others prefer a theory that understands fetishes as rituals. I discuss the consciousness or unconsciousness of fetishes, emphasizing that eliminating one fetish may give rise to new ones. Each individual has a unique perspective on fetishes and creates their own definition of this phenomenon.

In the introductory part of the magazine, I aim to familiarize readers with the issue of fetish and subsequently reflect on various perspectives that may exist. Sometimes, a fetish is perceived as a relationship with material objects. My personal belief is that every form of fetish has its legitimacy, and my effort lies in identifying those that resonate with me the most.

The "Fetish" magazine serves as a means to raise awareness that fetish/fetishism doesn't only encompass sexual expressions. Instead, it is a complex topic often tabooed in Czech society.
